# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and technically rigorous research community.

Participants must avoid harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, doxxing, threats, and deliberate disruption. Criticism of claims, methods, code, and experiments is welcome when it is specific and evidence-based.

Maintainers may edit or remove abusive content and restrict participation when necessary. Report conduct or security-sensitive concerns privately to `artur@msctechnology.com.br`.
